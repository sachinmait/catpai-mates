Course Modules

Core concepts including AI terminologies, machine learning, deep learning, LLMs, and Generative AI. Covers potential applications across dozens of industries and the role of data.

A deep dive into popular LLMs like ChatGPT, Gemini, and CoPilot. Introduces prompt engineering, context's role in output quality, and effective prompt templates.

Focuses on practical business applications: content writing, customer service automation, marketing, data analysis, supply chain management, legal documentation, and code generation.

Explore Al-powered design tools, video production with Deepfake technology, creative practices in journalism and art, branding, and developing educational materials.

Learn to integrate AI with existing systems, tailor models for specific industry needs, and understand Agentic AI. This module includes the capstone project design specifications.

Address critical topics like AI bias, intellectual property rights, regulatory compliance (DPDP, GDPR), and social damage risks. Features final capstone project demonstrations.
